Urmila Matondkar

Urmila Matondkar (born 4 February 1974), is an Indian actress, television personality and politician, primarily known for her work in Hindi cinema, in addition to Malayalam, Marathi, Tamil and Telugu films.

Mamta Kulkarni

Mamta Kulkarni is a former Indian actress and model. She was part of several commercial successful Hindi films such as Aashiq Awara, Waqt Hamara Hai, Krantiveer, Karan Arjun, Sabse Bada Khiladi, Baazi and China Gate among others.

Ayesha Jhulka

Ayesha Jhulka (born 28 July 1972) is an Indian film actress who predominantly appears in Hindi films. She has also worked in Odia, Kannada and Telugu languages films.
